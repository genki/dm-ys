Gem Maintainers

The DataMapper project consists several gems. dm-core is the main gem, and then with numerous plugins, known collectively as 'dm-more', as well as data_objects, which are the low level data-store drivers.

The gem maintainers should generally be your first point of contact if you have a patch you want introduced into the main line. They'll also be the person the bug is assigned to, at least initially, though they might not be the person who ultimately fixes it.
